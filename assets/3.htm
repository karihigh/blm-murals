  • bennys_journey

    “The BLM visibility team is a group of community members that came together around the issue of racial injustice in America. We felt that it was important to show our support for the Black Lives Matter movement in a visible and beautiful way in Sebastopol. We wanted to include the whole community in an art piece to show how much support there is in our town in a time of such great injustice. We took our idea and mock-up drawing to city council and were thrilled with the amount of support we got. There were a few people who didn’t agree with our message, but we couldn’t let them get us down. City council funded our project and in a short amount of time we started calculating our letters, making outlines, and painting! The amount of support from the community on the day of the event was astounding. It makes us feel so good that we brought together people of so many different backgrounds to create such an amazing art piece. All of these people are part of the change towards equality.” - @dezzerz
